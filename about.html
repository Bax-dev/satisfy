About Our Company

Our Mission

At Satisfy Retreats, we believe in the power of transformation and tranquility. Our retreats, set in the enchanting landscapes of The Gambia and the UK, are designed to rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit. We offer a unique blend of yoga, meditation, and immersive experiences to help you realign your focus and elevate your well-being

Our Founder

Founded by Melanie Allen, our retreats reflect her deep passion for yoga and self-development. Melanie’s personal journey through injury and growth inspired her to create a sanctuary for healing and self-discovery. Her dedication to expanding and enhancing the retreat experience ensures that every participant receives a transformative experience

Our Programs

Our meticulously curated programs provide a holistic approach to wellness, allowing you to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Whether you seek to deepen your spiritual journey or simply take a break to reset, our retreats offer a supportive environment for achieving balance and peace.

Experience Renewal

Join us to reconnect with yourself, enjoy nourishing homegrown cuisine, and embrace a renewed sense of vitality. At Satisfy Retreats, we are committed to guiding you on a transformative journey toward a more balanced and fulfilling life

How Satisfy can change your life

Our classes are suitable for all levels of experience, including beginners!

Retreats in West Africa

We organise cultural yoga retreats, offering a unique blend of relaxation, spiritual exploration through yoga, and immersion in African culture

Our Retreats

Our retreats provide participants with a rejuvenating getaway where they can discover the depths of spiritualism while delving into the richness of African traditions.

Many phases

Join Our Community

Experience the transformative power of yoga at Satisfy Yoga Studio. Join our community today and discover the joy of mindful movement, deep relaxation, and inner exploration

Client’s Feedback

Experience personalized attention and guidance with our private yoga sessions. Whether you're working with specific goals, recovering from an injury, or simply prefer one-on-one instruction, our private sessions offer tailored support to help you thrive in your practice.
